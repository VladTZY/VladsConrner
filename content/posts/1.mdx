Few films manage to be simultaneously devastating and uplifting. Shawshank does both effortlessly.

## What makes it work

The friendship between Andy and Red is the heart of everything. It's not a film about prison — it's about **what people do to survive**, and what hope costs.

> "Get busy living, or get busy dying."

Morgan Freeman's narration turns what could have been a generic thriller into something closer to a parable. You believe every word.

## A few things that stuck with me

- The rock hammer scene is perfect foreshadowing hiding in plain sight
- The library storyline earns its emotional payoff
- That ending — still hits every single time

One of those rare films you can return to and find something new each time.
